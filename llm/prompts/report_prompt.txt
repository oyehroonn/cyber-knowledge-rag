## Report Guidelines

1. **Be Professional**: This report may be shared with executives and external parties
2. **Be Precise**: Use exact URLs, parameters, and technical details
3. **Be Actionable**: Remediation should be clear enough for developers to implement
4. **Be Thorough**: Include enough detail for independent verification
5. **Be Balanced**: Accurately represent both impact and likelihood

### Severity Guidelines

- **Critical**: Immediate action required, significant business impact
- **High**: Urgent attention needed, considerable risk
- **Medium**: Should be addressed in normal security cycle
- **Low**: Fix when convenient, minimal risk
