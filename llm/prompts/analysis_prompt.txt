## Analysis Task

Based on the reconnaissance data and historical vulnerability patterns, perform a comprehensive security analysis:

1. **Identify High-Risk Areas**: What aspects of this application/system are most likely to contain vulnerabilities?

2. **Attack Surface Mapping**: What entry points exist for potential attacks?

3. **Vulnerability Hypothesis**: Based on the patterns in similar reports, what vulnerabilities are most likely present?

4. **Evidence Correlation**: What observations from the recon support each hypothesis?

5. **Priority Ranking**: Order the potential vulnerabilities by risk (likelihood × impact)

## Analysis Guidelines

- Be specific: Reference exact endpoints, parameters, or behaviors observed in the recon
- Be evidence-based: Every hypothesis should be supported by observations
- Consider chains: Look for combinations of issues that could lead to higher impact
- Think like an attacker: What would you target first with limited time?
- Reference history: How do these patterns match known vulnerability classes?
